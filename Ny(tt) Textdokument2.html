About Digital Product Pass

Digital Product Pass (DPP) is an innovative solution that aims to streamline the collection of information about the origin and manufacturing of clothing. By creating transparency in the fashion industry, the Product Passport enables consumers to understand the path of clothing from production to store. This increases awareness of sustainability and ethical issues, which encourages more informed purchasing decisions. The goal is to empower consumers and promote more responsible consumption in fashion.

Our Mission

To revolutionize product transparency in the fashion industry by providing comprehensive digital product passports that meet EU 2027 sustainability regulations while empowering consumers with the information they need to make responsible choices.

Key Features

  • Full material disclosure: Detailed information about all components and materials used
  • Supply chain transparency: Complete visibility of the production journey
  • Environmental impact data: Carbon footprint, water usage, and energy consumption
  • Certifications: Official sustainability certifications and standards
  • Care & recycling: Instructions for proper care and end-of-life handling

Compliance with EU Regulations

Our Digital Product Passport solution is designed to help businesses comply with upcoming EU regulations requiring detailed product information for sustainable goods. We stay ahead of regulatory changes to ensure our platform always meets the latest requirements.
